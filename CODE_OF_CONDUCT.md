# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project respectful, constructive, and focused on safe, lawful, user-consented automation.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and specific.
- Keep bug reports and examples free of private account data.
- Respect the no-login-automation policy.
- Help maintain a project culture that prioritizes privacy and safety.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment or abusive comments.
- Requests or contributions for credential collection, captcha bypass, account takeover, proxy/fingerprint/stealth behavior, or detection evasion.
- Posting passwords, cookies, tokens, session files, identity/payment data, private screenshots, or private listing queues.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove content that risks privacy or violates the safety policy, and may close requests that conflict with the project non-goals.
